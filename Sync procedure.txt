Create your repository via github.
Then login to your online account go to settings on your created repository, select collaborators.
Add your collaborators.

In the git hub program on your PC right click your repository and you can then select open in explorer.
This will locate your folder, and you can manually add files as you please.

THe procedure to commit change is to click your repository.

Commit requires title and description (click commit).
After you have commited you need to publish.
After your have published, you need to sync.

This should be done half daily with all collaborators to ensure good productivity.
Should there need be an early sync due to active simultaneous collaboration you will need to request someone synchronise earlier.
